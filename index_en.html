Bio-Recognition

The Trusense Bio Group is dedicated to developing highly specific and sensitive biosensing technologies, including biomarker detection and the synthesis of bio-recognition units. Works include creatinine-specific Molecularly Imprinted Polymers (MIPs), strand displacement aptamers, and more.

Instrument Development

The Trusense Instrument Group focuses on developing high-performance sensors adapted to bio-recognition units, as well as supporting detection algorithms. Works include the Trusense miniature electrochemical workstation and 3D-printed microneedle arrays.

Business Analysis

The Trusense Business Group is dedicated to analyzing the market translation potential of biosensing technologies. Works include the Trusense 2024 Business Canvas, market analysis, and risk analysis.

Art Design

The Art Design team is committed to providing professional visual designs for Trusense products. Works include Trusense 2024 product posters, product modeling, promotional posters, and videos.

Expert Guidance

Trusense receives continuous guidance and support from professionals in related fields. They come from different countries and various professional backgrounds, including biomedical engineering, biological sciences and technology, and finance.

We provide Tru-sensors

Innovative Design & Talented Development

The TruSense team is dedicated to developing rapid, convenient, and sensitive biosensor solutions. We focus on translating cutting-edge biosensing technologies into practical applications, providing innovative tools for early disease diagnosis and health monitoring. Our core strength lies in multidisciplinary innovation, combining microfluidics, electrochemical sensing, optical detection, and other technology platforms to develop portable detection devices with high specificity and sensitivity.

About Solution

Real-time, Convenient, Sensitive

Our team comprises members from diverse academic backgrounds, including biological sciences, medicine, biomedical engineering, computer science, and other related fields.

All experimental research is carried out at the Life and Health Innovation and Entrepreneurship Laboratory within the College of Life Sciences at Zhejiang University, with generous support and assistance from the Zhejiang University Biological Experiment Center, the College of Life Sciences, and the Zhejiang University Education Foundation.
